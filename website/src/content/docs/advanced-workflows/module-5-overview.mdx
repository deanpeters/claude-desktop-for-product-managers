Making your practice repeatable — for you, then for your team.

## What you'll cover

- Projects and memory: durable context so Claude stops starting from zero
- Skills: packaging a workflow so it runs the same way twice
- connectors: reaching the tools your team already lives in
- loops and goals: giving Claude an outcome and a cadence, with checkpoints you set
- quality-control patterns and review discipline
- packaging your workflow into something colleagues can adopt

## Why this comes before launch

Module 6 assumes this toolkit exists. Launch reporting, repeatable launch assets, pushing work into team tools — all of it leans on what you build here.
