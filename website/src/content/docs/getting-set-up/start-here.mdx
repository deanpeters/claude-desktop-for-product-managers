You don't need to be technical to take this course. This page walks you from nothing to your first lesson, one step at a time. If anything goes sideways, the fixes are at the bottom, and none of them are your fault.

## What you need

- The **Claude Desktop app**, installed from [claude.ai/download](https://claude.ai/download)
- A **Claude account** on a plan that includes Cowork

That's the whole list. No coding tools, no terminal, nothing else to install.

## Step 1: Get the course folder

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: browser download bar with the course ZIP, Mac and Windows variants */}

<Tabs>
  <TabItem label="Mac">
    1. [Download the course ZIP](https://github.com/deanpeters/claude-desktop-for-product-managers/archive/refs/heads/main.zip). One click, no account needed.
    2. Open your **Downloads** folder and double-click the ZIP. It unpacks into a folder named `claude-desktop-for-product-managers-main`, right next to it.
    3. Drag that folder somewhere you'll find again, like your Desktop or Documents.
  </TabItem>
  <TabItem label="Windows">
    1. [Download the course ZIP](https://github.com/deanpeters/claude-desktop-for-product-managers/archive/refs/heads/main.zip). One click, no account needed.
    2. Open your **Downloads** folder, right-click the ZIP, and choose **Extract All**, then **Extract**. You get a folder named `claude-desktop-for-product-managers-main`.
    3. Move that folder somewhere you'll find again, like your Desktop or Documents.
  </TabItem>
</Tabs>

Inside the folder you'll see `course-materials`. That's your classroom.

## Step 2: Point Claude at your classroom

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: Claude Desktop with Cowork visible; folder picker showing course-materials selected */}

1. Open Claude Desktop.
2. Start a **Cowork** session.
3. When it asks which folder to work in, choose the `course-materials` folder you just unpacked.

## Step 3: Say hello

{/* SCREENSHOT NEEDED: Cowork session with the start phrase pasted and Claude's Module 0 welcome reply */}

Paste this in, or just say hi in your own words. Claude knows why you're here the moment it sees the folder.

```text
I'm starting the course. Read COMPANION_INSTRUCTIONS.md and take me to Module 0.
```

The button in the corner of that box copies it for you.

From here, Claude takes over: it teaches, paces you, checks your work, and remembers where you left off between sessions. And any time you feel lost, just say **"I'm lost"**. Claude stops, re-orients you, and offers a way forward. No judgment, no penalty.

## Stuck before you even started?

Paste this into any Claude chat, even on your phone, and Claude becomes your setup guide:

```text
I'm a product manager setting up the "Claude Desktop for Product Managers"
course from https://github.com/deanpeters/claude-desktop-for-product-managers
Ask me whether I'm on Mac or Windows, then walk me through it one step at
a time: downloading the course ZIP from that page, unzipping it, finding
the course-materials folder inside it, opening a Cowork session in Claude
Desktop, and pointing it at that folder. Check each step worked before
moving to the next.
```

## Common snags

- **"I don't see Cowork in Claude Desktop."** Update the app to the latest version, and check that your plan includes Cowork. If it doesn't, that's the one upgrade this course needs.
- **"I can't find the unzipped folder."** It's almost always still in Downloads, sitting next to the ZIP. Search your computer for `course-materials` if it's hiding.
- **"Claude is acting like a coding assistant, not a teacher."** You probably pointed the session at the wrong folder level. Start a new Cowork session and choose the `course-materials` folder itself, then say "I'm here for the course."
